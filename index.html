Sehej Home Care

Sehej is an In- home care service for all the elders who needs a helping hand and family members who still wants to look after thier elderly's but have to compensate work and family responsibilities. In-home senior care is THE option in such cases A home care service like ours will provide support to the elderly who are vulnerable while still delegating them to live an independent life at home. We help seniors age in place by providing them with the right care, comfort and connection sehej provides high caliber fostering for adults and seniors who needs assitance in the daily living. In addition,we bring forth companion care from our companions for elders who require a more personalized touch. We offer caregiving in a warm, safe environment. Our caregivers will be triple screened for compassion, character and competence. Sehej supports older adults, those with disabilities or limited mobility, and the elderly in need of companionship or caregiving. A platform for elders to socialize and share their interests. We're all about bridging the gaps in society. In a nutshell a professional, compassionate and loving care for elderly people and their families in your community.
