About Us

The starting point for starting this venture/startup was an observation about my grandmother. I always remember her as an active, independent personality in the family. Actively participating in the household and outside in her own social circle. Someone who always had a schedule of the day planned various outings with us grandchildren and traveled places on her own. In the recent year though, we noticed a change in her behavior. She'd grown extremely silent and laconic, spent most of her time in her own room, hardly engaged in family activity, and isolated herself from all social interaction.To understand elders in a better way we had a conversation with some relatives and neighbors who helped us understand the lives of older adults, and gave us insight about their aspirations and the problems they encounter in their daily life.
