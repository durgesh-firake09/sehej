Our Services

After the age of 60, health risks increases & may develop various health issues that will conditionally make them weak and liable. They need regular monitoring, medications, and sometimes emergency assistance.

The four major old age problems include:-

Physical problems

Emotional problems

Social problems

Cognitive problems

The caregivers will make sure to indulge the elders in the activities such as exercising, meditating, socializing, etc. to deal with the above problems mentioned. If in case of emergency, since our caregivers are nurses, they will ensure to assist them and handle the situation conscientiously

>

Helping with Daily chores

The aim of Sehej is to support the elderly by helping with their daily chores. Self-care activities that would be performed include personal hygiene, grooming, bathing, toileting and dressing will be assisted by our caregivers.

Ensuring their safety

We offer live-in staff, who will provide your loved one with a safe and secure environment in which they can enjoy the company, while you get back to your own everyday routine.

Quality of Living

Sehej will not only ensure healthcare but also emotional support. Working children or those who are living away from home can lead to insufficient interaction with their parents, leaving the elders with no one to talk to. Sehej companions will try and make their life content full.

Lack of Companionship

Most senior citizens who probably live alone suffer due to lack of companionship sometimes exacerbated by lack of mobility due to ill health. Loneliness and isolation are definitely a major concern among elderly Indians who are above the age of 60. Isolation basically can result in gradual depression and other mental disorders in the elderly. our caregivers will try to develop a strong bond with older parents and engage them in thier and eventually they will make thier life easier.
